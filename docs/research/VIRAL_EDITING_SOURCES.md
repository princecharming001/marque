# Viral editing research — sources for knowledge/viral_editing.md

Collected 2026-07 via Firecrawl web research to ground `backend/knowledge/viral_editing.md`.

## Primary data
- **OpusClip — B-Roll & Visual Effects Guide for Short-Form Video (2026 Data)**
  https://www.opus.pro/research/broll-visual-effects-short-form
  Analysis of 13.5M clips. Key figures used:
  - B-Roll present in **6.0%** of clips (733,762) — #1 visual enhancement after captions.
  - Transitions 2.4%, Text overlays 4.5%, Outro 4.6%, Intro 1.0%, **Visual hooks 0.04%**
    (4,806 clips) — rare = strong differentiator.
  - Practitioner rules: b-roll 3–5s max; pull from ±1.5s around the keyword; J-cut/L-cut;
    cut on motion; match resolution; faces get ~28% more early retention; close-ups win
    the first 3s; don't cover punchlines; keep intros <2s; text on-screen first 2s.

## Pacing / speech rate
- Video Production Stack Exchange — Optimal pace of speech (≈150 wpm target; 120–200 wpm
  conversational). https://video.stackexchange.com/questions/10390/optimal-pace-of-speech
- Prezent — words per minute in a speech (slow 100–120 / normal 130–160 / fast 170–200).
  https://www.prezent.ai/blog/words-per-minute-speech
- "How to Calculate Your Speech Speed (retention)" — speak clearly, speed up WPM in post
  to ~165–175 without changing natural delivery. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3HK40OxMGno

## Sequencing / retention
- Vibesdrop — Short-Form Video Sequencing Techniques (1-2-3 build: hook in first 3s,
  build, payoff; cut on the beat; AIDA). https://vibesdrop.com/sequencing-techniques-short-form-videos/
- Practitioner consensus (IG/TikTok editor reels): remove dead pauses + "umms", zoom cuts
  for emphasis, captions always on, hook in first 3 seconds.

## Captions
- Zeely — captions for video content (readable mobile font, high contrast, limit words
  per line, active-word emphasis for watch time).
  https://zeely.ai/blog/how-to-make-captions-for-video-content-fast-and-accurate/
- Riverside — 7 steps to edit a talking-head (remove mistakes from transcript, cut
  silences, remove fillers, add captions). https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m-18bJSouYI
